Predicting the 2018 Senate Midterm Election

In recent years, the frequent occurrence of black swan events in politics have made it increasingly difficult to predict election outcomes through solely traditional means (e.g. polling). The unprecedented result of the 2016 U.S. presidential election and the 2016 Brexit referendum in the United Kingdom both emphasized the importance of exploring alternative ways to predict elections.

In predicting the results of the 2018 Senate midterm election, we will use 1) machine learning on economic and demographic data and 2) sentiment analysis on tweets prior to election day to determine which party will win. Our two-pronged model and its methodologies are illustrated below.
